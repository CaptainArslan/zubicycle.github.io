ZuBicycle!

To use the ZU Bicycle Sharing System, passengers need to register as members following TransPeshawar's policy. They can activate their bicycles using the ZU Card/ZU App and register using their National Identity Card or Passport. A refundable security deposit is required for membership. Ensure your ZU Card or ZU App has a minimum balance of Rs.20 before starting your journey. To release a bicycle, tap your Fare Media at the docks, and when docking, tap your ZU Card again. Remember to tap both at entry and exit. Failure to do so may result in your card being blocked. For ZU Mobile App users, a tap at entry is sufficient. The system tracks your time and deducts the fare accordingly. If your balance is insufficient, you can dock the bicycle with the remaining amount as a one-time loan.
